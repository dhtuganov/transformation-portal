### 3. ROI Calculation Framework

**Simple ROI:**
```
ROI = (Total Benefits - Total Costs) / Total Costs Ã— 100%

Benefits (3 years): $400K
Costs (3 years): $180K
ROI = ($400K - $180K) / $180K = 122%
```

**Payback Period:**
```
Payback = Total Investment / Annual Benefit

Example:
Investment: $150K
Annual benefit: $120K
Payback = $150K / $120K = 1.25 years (15 months)
```

**NPV (Net Present Value):**
```
NPV = Î£ (Benefit_year / (1 + discount_rate)^year) - Investment

Year 1: $120K / 1.1 = $109K
Year 2: $120K / 1.21 = $99K
Year 3: $120K / 1.33 = $90K
```
